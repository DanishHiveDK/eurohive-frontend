Terms of Service

Last updated: April 2026 — Effective for all users of eurohive.eu

1. Company Information

Eurohive is operated by Eurohive, a company registered in Denmark with CVR number 34808090. Registered address: Denmark. Contact: hello@eurohive.eu.

2. Platform Overview

Eurohive is a freelance marketplace connecting clients (“Clients”) with independent freelancers (“Freelancers”) across the European Union. Eurohive acts solely as an intermediary; it is not a party to any contract between Clients and Freelancers.

3. Account Registration & Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and legally able to enter contracts in your country. You agree to provide accurate information during registration. Eurohive reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these Terms.

4. HoneyDrops (HD) — Platform Credits

HoneyDrops (HD) are internal platform credits used by Freelancers to place bids on jobs and promote their services. HD are not currency, have no monetary value outside the platform, and are non-refundable. HD are purchased via Stripe. HD balances may expire if an account is inactive for 12 months.

5. Jobs, Bids & Contracts

Clients post jobs describing scope, timeline, and budget. Freelancers submit bids (spending HD) with a cover letter and proposed EUR amount. A contract is formed when a Client accepts a bid. Clients must fund the EUR escrow before accepting any bid. Eurohive’s milestone-based contract system allows Clients to release payment per agreed milestone.

6. Payments & EUR Escrow

All payments are processed via Stripe in EUR. When a Client accepts a bid, the agreed contract amount is held in escrow. Funds are released to the Freelancer upon Client approval of each milestone. Eurohive charges a platform fee based on a loyalty staircase — the fee decreases automatically as your total earnings on the platform grow. The fee is deducted from the escrow at payout. VAT may apply based on your jurisdiction. Payouts are made directly to the Freelancer’s verified bank account via Stripe Connect.

7. Cancellation & Refunds

If a job is cancelled before work begins, the Client may request a refund of the escrowed amount (minus any Stripe processing fees). HD spent on bids are non-refundable. Disputes between Clients and Freelancers are handled via Eurohive’s dispute resolution process.

8. Platform Communications

All communication between Clients and Freelancers must take place within the Eurohive messaging system. Sharing contact information (email, phone, social media) to bypass the platform is prohibited and may result in account suspension.

9. KYC & Identity Verification

Freelancers are required to complete identity verification (KYC) before withdrawing earnings. Eurohive uses third-party KYC providers compliant with EU AML directives. Incomplete KYC may restrict account features.

10. GDPR & Data Protection

Eurohive processes personal data in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Data is stored on EU infrastructure. You have the right to access, rectify, and erase your data. For data requests, contact hello@eurohive.eu.

11. Prohibited Conduct

You may not: use the platform for illegal purposes; submit false information; engage in fraudulent transactions; attempt to reverse-engineer the platform; or interfere with other users’ accounts.

12. Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by Danish law, Eurohive’s liability is limited to fees paid in the 3 months preceding the claim. Eurohive is not liable for disputes between Clients and Freelancers, lost profits, or indirect damages.

13. Governing Law

These Terms are governed by the laws of Denmark. Disputes are subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of Danish courts.

14. Changes to Terms
